The project was developed as the final form of a course of study in Grafica Computazionale, held by prof. A. Paoluzzi.

In the first part, we were taught how to draw straight lines and create 3D objects from them; then we moved on to face

curved lines; the final project represents the bones of a hand, that unlike other objects are composed of irregular curves

and surfaces. Below you can find the whole documentation I used to draw the bones and some screens made of the model.
