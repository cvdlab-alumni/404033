The fingers of the hand are made from the metacarpals and phalanges; the metacarpals are the palm of the hand; phalanges

represents the fingers: structurally similar to metacarpal bones (therefore they have been represented with the same model),

with the exception of last phalanges which have a different form. Below there are images of hand's bones on which I based my

model.
